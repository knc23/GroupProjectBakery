OUR STORY

Petal bakes was created with a simple idea: to make freshly-baked high quality treats more accessible and convenient for everyone. Inspired by the comfort and joy that baked goods bring, our bakery was founded to combine traditional baking with the ease of online ordering. We believe that everyone should be able to enjoy delicious, handcrafted pastries without having to leave the comfort of your own home

What started as a passion for baking has grown into a place where creativity, flavour and quality come together.At petal bakes every item is carefully prepared to bring warmth and comfort to your day.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to deliver hand-crafted baked goods directly to your doorsteps with just a few clicks. You can enjoy these mouth-watering delights all baked with care, intricacy and love
